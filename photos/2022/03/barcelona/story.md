A March business trip to **Barcelona**. The conference kept the days full, so
the photography happened indoors — the hotel turned out to be all warm light
and quiet, considered detail.

## DSC_0547
Woven-linen pendants over the lobby, each one a little sun behind a burlap
screen. Shot wide open, close, letting the texture go soft.

## DSC_0566
A reading lamp in the room, late. The whole frame is one warm falloff — the
kind of light you don't photograph so much as sit inside.
