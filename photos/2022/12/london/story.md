A December business trip to **London**, with an afternoon stolen for the
**Natural History Museum** — from a killer bird's skull to the great whale
hanging in the hall.

## DSC_5499
The skull of a *Phorusrhacos*, an extinct carnivorous "killer bird", behind
glass — that enormous hooked beak catching the low light of the case.

## DSC_5527
"Hope", the blue whale skeleton diving through **Hintze Hall**, shot straight
up the spine into the museum's cathedral of iron and terracotta. (Lifted a
stop-and-a-bit in post — it was darker than the eye remembered.)
