title: Red Bull Showrun, Belgrade
---
The **Red Bull Racing Showrun** came to **Belgrade** — Formula 1 and Red Bull's
drift BMWs running a wet downtown street, thousands of phones held up in the
rain. A grey day that the liveries lit up anyway.

## DSC_3207
Two of the Red Bull **BMW M4** drift cars nose-to-tail through the bus-lane
markings, exhausts stacked, the crowd pinned behind the barriers.

## DSC_3572
One M4 hooked into a drift, throwing a sheet of spray off the wet cobbles — the
whole street leaning in to watch.

## DSC_3601
The one everyone came for: the **Red Bull F1 car**, wet tyres on, wailing down
the straight between the crash barriers.

## DSC_3939
The F1 car head-on, umbrellas up the whole length of the barrier — everyone soaked, nobody leaving.
